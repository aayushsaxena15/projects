About

Project X provides the opportunity to the developers to interact with people by enabling them to upload their projects in the fields of games,mobiles etc.

People can view the public projects and experience a different world by taking an insight of the project.They can also comment,rate and download a project.

The developers can update their projects and also provide a manual which will tell the users how to operate the project.For creating a project, you must be a registered user.
